What is a Webring?

A webring is a collection of websites that are linked together. It allows users to easily navigate between related websites by clicking on "Next" or "Previous" links, forming a virtual ring of interconnected sites. Webrings were popular in the early days of the internet. They are a really good alternative to social networks, as they are a lot more personal and allow for more creativity.
